### Ingredients

* for 200 g plate
  - 150 g chickpeas
  - 40 g tahini
  - 1 glove garlic
  - one lemon juice
  - tea spoon ground cumin
  - olive oil

### Method

  1- puree all ingredients, except for the chickpeas, until they form a smooth paste. 
  2- Add the chickpeas, then puree the mixture until it is smooth.
